About Me

I'm Jaisree M K, an able, enthusiastic, and reliable data science student currently pursuing M.Sc. Data Science at Christ University, Bangalore. I am deeply passionate about leveraging data to solve real-world problems and creating intelligent systems that make a meaningful impact. My journey in data science has equipped me with strong skills in Machine Learning, Deep Learning, and Generative AI. I have worked on diverse projects ranging from VLM for Remote Sensing to Track Sense for railway defect detection, and Crop Yield Prediction systems. I am also actively involved in research, having published survey papers on synthetic data generation and crop-weed classification. What drives me is the combination of technical excellence, creativity, and the ability to follow through on ambitious goals. I thrive in collaborative environments and enjoy taking on leadership roles, whether as Team Lead for projects or as IEEE Student Branch Secretary. My dedication extends beyond academics to sports and extracurricular activities, where I've excelled as Captain of the Throwball team and as an athlete.

Believing that with dedication and innovation, we can bring great changes and make the world a better place to live in!
